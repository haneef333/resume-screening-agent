Job Title: Data Scientist

We are looking for a Data Scientist with at least 2 years of experience to join our
analytics team. The ideal candidate will build and deploy machine learning models,
analyze large datasets, and communicate insights to stakeholders.

Required Skills:
- Python, Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn
- SQL for data querying
- Machine Learning and Statistics
- Data Visualization (Matplotlib/Seaborn/Tableau)
- Experience with TensorFlow or PyTorch is a plus
- Familiarity with AWS or GCP
- Strong communication skills

Responsibilities:
- Build predictive models for customer analytics
- Design and maintain ETL pipelines
- Present findings to non-technical stakeholders
- Collaborate with engineering teams on model deployment
